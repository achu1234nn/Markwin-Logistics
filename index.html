About Markwin Logistics

Your licensed customs broker & cold-chain logistics partner

Markwin Logistics India Private Limited is a full-service logistics and customs clearance company operating across major ports, airports, ICDs and FTWZ facilities throughout India.

As a Licensed Customs Broker, we provide specialized solutions for the import, storage, movement and regulatory clearance of medical devices, diagnostic systems, hospital infrastructure and precision components.

We build long-term partnerships through efficiency, transparency and value-driven service — from first HS classification to last-mile hospital delivery.
